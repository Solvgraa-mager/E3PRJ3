actor SumoBot_p1 #green
actor SumoBot_p2 #green

CC --> SumoBot_p1 : Connected
deactivate SumoBot_p1

CC -> Bruger : Display("SumoBot 1: Connected") 

SumoBot_p2 -> CC : ConnectToCC()
activate SumoBot_p2
CC --> SumoBot_p2 : Connected
deactivate SumoBot_p2
CC -> Bruger : Display("SumoBot 2: Connected") 

== State: Ready == 
CC -> CC : WaitForStartSignal()
